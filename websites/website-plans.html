What is the goal of office hours?  

For non-Monthly Website Clients, I estimate the work, have them approve and I answer any questions via email, and then I complete the work on their website. For Monthly Website clients that need a helping hand each month, You get a face-to-face discussion or Skype call, to allow you to really get the attention you need on what needs to be done with your site. Overall, it is a more hands-on approach and allows you to describe EXACTLY what you need.

Can you explain the 10% discount on estimates?

If you need something new that is not included as an eligible task under the Monthly Website Plan, as a Plan Holder - you would get 10% off on the estimate of what I normally charge non-monthly website plan holders.

For example, if you came to me requesting a project for me to build say a new"mini" site for a new product or service you are offering and I estimated that would cost, hypothetically, $1000.00 - you, as a Monthly Website Plan holder would get a 10% discount: and the work would only be $900.00 (versus $1K for everyone else who is not a Monthly Website Plan Holder.)

It's a benefit for you if you know you will need me to create some big projects for you in the future.
